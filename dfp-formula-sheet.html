M/M/1 Single-Server Queue

Symbols. $\lambda$ = arrival rate;   $\mu$ = service rate;   $\rho = \lambda/\mu$ = utilization. Stability requires $\lambda < \mu$.
MeasureSymbolFormula
Utilization$\rho$$\lambda/\mu$
Avg. number in system$L$$\rho/(1-\rho)$
Avg. time in system$W$$1/(\mu-\lambda)$
Avg. number in queue$L_q$$\rho^{2}/(1-\rho)$
Avg. wait in queue$W_q$$\lambda/[\mu(\mu-\lambda)]$

Little's Law

$L = \lambda \cdot W \qquad L_q = \lambda \cdot W_q$

Basic EOQ Model

Symbols. $D$ = annual demand;   $S$ = ordering cost per order;   $H$ = holding cost per unit per year;   $W$ = working days per year.
$Q^{*}=\sqrt{\dfrac{2DS}{H}} \qquad TC^{*}_{\text{order}+\text{hold}}=\sqrt{2DSH}$
$N=\dfrac{D}{Q^{*}} \qquad T=\dfrac{W}{N}=\dfrac{Q^{*}}{D}\cdot W$

Reorder Point (constant lead time)

$d=\dfrac{D}{W} \qquad \text{ROP}=d\cdot L$

EOQ with Purchase Cost

$TC_{\text{total}}(Q)=\dfrac{D}{Q}\cdot S + \dfrac{Q}{2}\cdot H + D\cdot P$

Economic Production Quantity (EPQ)

When a firm produces and consumes simultaneously at production rate $p$ (units/day) and usage rate $d$ (units/day), with $p>d$:
$Q_{p}^{*}=\sqrt{\dfrac{2DS}{H\bigl(1-d/p\bigr)}} \qquad I_{\max}=Q_{p}^{*}\Bigl(1-\dfrac{d}{p}\Bigr)$
$TC=\dfrac{D}{Q_{p}^{*}}\cdot S + \dfrac{I_{\max}}{2}\cdot H$

Sensitivity (Square-Root Rule)

Both $Q^{*}$ and $TC^{*}$ are proportional to $\sqrt{D}$.
Demand multiplier$Q^{*}$ and $TC^{*}$ multiplier% change
$\times 2$$\times \sqrt{2} \approx 1.414$$+41.4\%$
$\times 4$$\times 2$$+100\%$
$\times 0.5$$\times \sqrt{0.5} \approx 0.707$$-29.3\%$

Simple Exponential Smoothing

$F_{t+1} = \alpha \cdot A_{t} + (1-\alpha)\cdot F_{t}$
Equivalently:   $F_{t+1} = F_{t} + \alpha\cdot(A_{t}-F_{t})$

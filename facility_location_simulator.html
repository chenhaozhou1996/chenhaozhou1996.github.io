üéØ Key Managerial Insights

Fixed vs. Variable Cost Trade-off

Opening fewer facilities reduces fixed costs but increases transportation costs and may reduce service levels. The optimal network balances these competing factors based on demand density and cost structure.

Coverage vs. Efficiency

100% coverage often requires disproportionate investment in low-demand areas. Strategic under-coverage of marginal customers can significantly reduce total costs while maintaining high average service levels.

Capacity Utilization Balance

High utilization maximizes asset efficiency but leaves no buffer for demand variability. The optimal utilization target depends on demand uncertainty and the cost of stockouts or delayed service.

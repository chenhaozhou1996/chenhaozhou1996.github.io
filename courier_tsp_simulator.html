üéØ Key Managerial Insights

The Last-Mile Challenge

Last-mile delivery often represents 50%+ of total logistics costs. Efficient routing directly impacts profitability, customer satisfaction through shorter delivery windows, and environmental sustainability through reduced fuel consumption.

Heuristics vs. Optimality

For practical problem sizes (20+ stops), exact optimal solutions are computationally infeasible. Well-designed heuristics like 2-opt typically achieve within 5% of optimal while running in seconds rather than hours.

Dynamic Routing Evolution

Modern logistics uses real-time traffic data, dynamic customer requests, and machine learning to continuously re-optimize routes. The static TSP is a foundational building block for these advanced systems.
